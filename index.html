📱 Add to Home Screen

🍎 iPhone/iPad (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Edit the name if desired, then tap "Add"
  4. The app icon will appear on your home screen

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the Menu button (three dots) in the top right
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  3. Edit the name if desired, then tap "Add"
  4. The app icon will appear on your home screen

🖥️ Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

  1. Look for the Install icon in the address bar
  2. Or click the Menu button (three dots)
  3. Click "Install Tiger Document Library"
  4. The app will be installed as a desktop application
💡 Benefits: Once installed, you can access your documents offline, get faster loading times, and launch the app directly from your home screen without opening a browser.
